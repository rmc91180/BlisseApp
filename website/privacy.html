Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Your privacy is our highest priority. We understand the sensitive nature of Blisse and have built it to protect your information.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Your personal data including preferences, favorites, notes, activity history, and settings are stored ONLY on your device using secure local storage.

  • We do NOT have servers storing your personal information
  • Most activity data stays on your phone
  • Authentication and submitted support messages are sent only to required processors
  • Deleting the App permanently removes all personal data

Authentication Services

Sign-in is handled by Apple Sign-In and Firebase Authentication (Google). We do not store passwords or access your credentials directly. These services have their own privacy policies.

Anonymous Analytics

We use PostHog for anonymous, aggregated analytics to improve the App. This includes:

  • Which features are most popular
  • General usage patterns
  • App performance metrics

We disable person profiling and send only sanitized event metadata. Free-text notes and support messages are never sent to analytics.

Information We NEVER Collect

  • Biometric data (Face ID/Touch ID is handled by your device's secure enclave)
  • Your location
  • Your free-text notes or support messages in analytics streams
  • Photos, contacts, or any other personal files
  • Any sale of your personal information

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) - for secure sign-in
  • Apple Sign-In - for iOS authentication
  • PostHog - for anonymous analytics
  • Formspree - for support messages you submit

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

Data Retention

Your local data persists until you delete it via Settings or uninstall the App. Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 24 months to analyze long-term trends.

Children's Privacy

Blisse is strictly for adults 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we learn we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Since your personal data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View your data anytime in the App
  • Deletion: Delete all data via Settings → Reset Data
  • Portability: Your data exists only on your device

For EU residents (GDPR) and California residents (CCPA), you may contact us to exercise additional rights regarding any data we may process.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices. Local data is protected by your device's security. The optional PIN lock uses secure storage. Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
