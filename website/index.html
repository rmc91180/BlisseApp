About Blisse

We are building the app we wanted for ourselves: less awkward advice, more real connection.

From one real couple to another 💞

Blisse began with a simple question: why does keeping the spark alive feel harder than it should?

After too many generic date lists and forgettable ideas, we built a playful relationship playground where couples can explore, laugh, and reconnect with intention.

No pressure, no perfection. Just practical intimacy tools that make your relationship feel alive, warm, and fun again.
